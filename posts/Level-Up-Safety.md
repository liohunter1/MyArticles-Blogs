## What is a "Gaming Footprint"?

When we talk about a digital footprint, we usually think of social media posts or search history. In gaming, it looks a little different. It's the trail of data left behind by:

* **Chat logs** (text and voice)  
* **Usernames** that might reveal too much  
* **In-game purchases** linked to financial data  
* **Friend lists** and social connections
